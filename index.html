Hey, I'm Zach.

I'm an award-winning dancer who travels around the world teaching dance clinics. I've been a round 4 finalist on FOX TV's popular show "So You Think You Can Dance" and most recently, I've worked with Quest Crew on LMFAO's new music video "poppin' bottles".
I'd love to work with you, whether it be teaching a clinic at your studio or something else, let's connect.
Workshops
Typical Offerings
Classes by Age Group
We often seperate this two hour class into ages 5-11 and then ages 12 and up. This class is taught for people of any experience, though I can tailor it for your specific studio as needed.
"So you think you can dance" experience
Ages 11 and up : Students go through a mock audition, choreography, just like the TV show. This class will teach them how to make good impressions on judges and improve their stage presence and performance. Anyone is welcome to take it! All styles and levels, but more for your competitive dancers.
Details and Availability
Currently, I am teaching dance all over Asia. I will be headed back to America in mid-August and will be there until December. I have a 10 student minimum for dance clinics and pricing depends on the type of clinic offered.

Please feel free to get in touch with me for more information about booking a clinic, pricing, and any other questions you may have by using the nearby form.
